# Week 1 – Familiarization with the Existing Platform

## Objectives

- Gain practical familiarity with exploring the platform;
- Identify key functionalities and their current scope;
- Collect and organize relevant documentation for future reference.

During this stage, the goal is to develop hands-on experience with the platform, understanding how data flows through its modules (e.g., data collection, processing and storage). This also involves taking note of technical limitations, usability issues or gaps that may later inform the research direction.
